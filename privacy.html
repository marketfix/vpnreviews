VPNReview PRO privacy policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

VPNReview PRO publishes informational content about VPN services. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the site or contact the site by email.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, VPNReview PRO or its hosting providers may process routine technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, referrer data, timestamps, requested pages, and basic diagnostic logs. This information is used for site delivery, security, abuse prevention, and aggregate performance monitoring.

Cookies and similar technologies

The site may use standard cookies or local storage required for basic site function, performance measurement, or link attribution. The site does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information through public-facing forms.

Email and direct contact

If you email admin@vpnreview.pro, the site may process your name, email address, message content, and any information you choose to include. That information is used to answer the request, handle corrections, review legal or rights notices, and maintain communication records.

Affiliate links

Some links on the site may be affiliate links. If you click one of those links, the destination provider or affiliate platform may use cookies, tracking parameters, or related attribution tools to record that click. Their own privacy policies govern that processing once you leave VPNReview PRO.

How information is used

Information may be used to operate the website, maintain security, understand aggregate usage, answer messages, investigate abuse, comply with legal obligations, and document editorial corrections or business records.

Sharing

Information may be shared with service providers that support hosting, email, analytics, security, or affiliate tracking, but only as needed to operate the site. Information may also be disclosed if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of the site.

Retention

Technical logs and business records may be kept for as long as reasonably necessary for security, operational, legal, tax, and recordkeeping purposes. Email correspondence may be retained as needed to manage the communication or maintain an audit trail.

Your choices

You can limit cookie use through browser controls and avoid sending personal information by email unless necessary. If you want to request deletion of a direct email communication record, contact admin@vpnreview.pro. Some information may still be retained where there is a legal or legitimate business reason to do so.

Children

The site is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will change when material updates are published.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to admin@vpnreview.pro.
